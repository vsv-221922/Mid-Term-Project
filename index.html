How to use SpeechPad

  • Click "Start Listening" to begin voice recognition
  • Speak clearly into your microphone
  • Your speech will appear in the text box in real-time
  • Click "Stop Listening" when you're done speaking
  • Click "Save as Note" to store your text for later
  • You can edit the text manually before saving
